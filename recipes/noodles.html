Burger

Noodles, a culinary delight cherished across cultures, offer a symphony of flavors and textures in every tantalizing bite. Crafted from flour, water, and sometimes eggs, these versatile strands of goodness come in various shapes and sizes, each lending its own unique charm to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vegetables
  • Sauces
  • Vinegar

Steps to Make the Dish

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, salt, and water, then let it rest.

  2. Roll out the dough: Divide and flatten into thin sheets.

  3. Cut the noodles: Slice the dough into thin strips.

  4. Cook the noodles: Boil in salted water until tender.

  5. Serve and enjoy: Drain, rinse, and serve with your favorite sauce or toppings.
